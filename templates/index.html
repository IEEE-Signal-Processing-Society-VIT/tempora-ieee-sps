ABOUT IEEE-SPS-VIT

Founded as IEEE’s first society in 1948, the Signal Processing Society is the world’s premier association for signal processing engineers and industry professionals. IEEE-SPS VIT Chapter is one of the leading and finest student chapters in VIT University. We conduct webinars, classroom sessions for hands-on experience, night lab sessions and outreach in Vellore. Our primary vision is to provide the innovative and enthusiastic 'engineers-to-be' a platform to explore their ideas and knowledge in t he Signal Processing field. We aim at making students apply their knowledge into real-life, productive projects; hereby creating progress in the scientific arena

Let's Introduce you to TEMPORA

TEMPORA

Tempora 2.0 is a week long ideathon conducted by IEEE SPS. There are 5 technical themes and 5 problem statements for the participants to choose to ideate on. A form link will be shared for submission of innovative ideas in form of a documentation. An ideathon and the perfect platform for your imagination skills where a creative and unique idea is enough to make you win. 'All achievements, all earned reaches have their beginning in an idea' - Napolean Hill

Domains

Signal Processing

Robotics Process Automation

Problem Statement 1

Reducing food wastage by monitoring/optimizing supply chain

Problem Statement 2

Signal processing to enable secure communications for military applications

Problem Statement 3

Design/deployment of a secure blockchain based voting system

Problem Statement 4

Using AR/VR/ MR for immersive teaching

Problem Statement 5

Personal identity Security using cyber security or blockchain

Instructions

  • Participants have to submit documentation of an innovative idea.
  • Idea will be judged on the basis of its novelty, creativity and feasibility.
  • Team size 1-3.
  • For Submission here in this website, click on Register Button and fill the information accordingly.
  • Providing correct details of all members of your group is mandatory for generating certificates.
  • Idea submission starts on 12th July and ends on 19th July.

Details to be filled

  • Title of idea.
  • Abstract.
  • Objective.
  • Novelty.
  • Tech stack.
  • Circuit/block diagram (upload your document in your google drive and share the link here, make sure it is visible to all).
  • Hardware implementation
  • Business strategy
